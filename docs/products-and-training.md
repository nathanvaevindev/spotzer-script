## Selling Websites & Digital Marketing

### Core Philosophy
This isn't about tech — it's about **business outcomes**.

- Focus on goals, problems, and growth — not jargon
- Start with **low-hanging fruit**: no website / older than 2 years / not mobile-friendly / outdated or broken
- Be clear about what you're selling — talk to benefits and simplicity
- Position as **service + support**, not just a product

> **Say:** *"We're not just building a site — we're building trust and visibility."*

### Market Opportunity
- **$63.6B** in online sales in Australia in 2023
- **8 in 10** Australian households shopped online in 2023
- **98%** of DIY/amateur websites fail — professionals are eventually sought
- **43%** of small businesses plan to invest in their website performance

### Website Red Flags — Help customers see what's holding them back
- Slow loading or broken pages
- Non-mobile responsive
- Confusing navigation and structure
- Spelling mistakes, ALL CAPS text
- Wrong or outdated info (address, phone, hours)
- No integration of modern tools (bookings, live chat)
- Poor first impression = missed opportunity

> **Ask:** *"When was the last time you looked at your site through your customer's eyes? Does it answer the right questions? Do you shop on outdated websites?"*

### Leading Great Sales Conversations
- Build rapport — people buy from those they like
- Uncover customer needs and aspirations
- Identify pain points like confusing navigation or poor site structure
- Paint the picture: *"What could your business look like with a modern site working for you?"*
- Show tangible impact — more leads, better reputation, fewer missed customers
- Balance listening with sharing your expertise

> **Say:** *"We help you get found, look good, and grow — on every device."*

### Objection Handling — Website & Digital Marketing

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "A friend is doing it" | Will they support it long-term? Handle SEO, updates, and integrations? |
| "No time" | We do everything for you — design, content, setup, and ongoing support. |
| "I already have a nice site" | Is it mobile-friendly? When was it last updated? Does it reflect your brand now? |
| "I'm a small business" | That's exactly why it matters — a great site builds trust from the first click. |
| "I only need Facebook" | Social is important, but you don't own that platform. Your website is your asset. |
| "I did it myself" | Impressive! Now let's take it to the next level professionally and build in SEO best practice. |
| "Maybe next year" | What would waiting cost in missed customers or trust? |
| "I've never needed a website" | That worked before. Now people check online first — even for referrals. |

**Script Prompts:**
- "Did you know Google deprioritized websites that aren't mobile-friendly?"
- "Over 65% of searches are made on mobile — and even higher in metro areas."
- "We build agency-quality websites that work on every device — and we do it all for you."
- "A great website is your most important digital asset. We make it easy."

## Creating a Lead in Salesforce

1. Go to the Leads section in Salesforce
2. Select **New**
3. Choose lead source: **Online Essentials AU**
4. Fill in mandatory fields
5. Add all relevant notes and business details
6. Ensure you have: customer full name, phone, and email
7. Generate the lead — **set status to New**, then once created **convert**
8. Add further sales details: ABN, address, etc. & select product sold
9. Convert the lead
10. **Book call with CM**

*If creating a lead: select **OE APAC Upsells***

## Sales Process — Quote (Existing Customer)

*(Use when customer will not agree on the spot and needs time to review — not a sale yet)*

**1. Chargebee**
- Open Chargebee → find the customer → create a quote
- Send quote and have customer agree to terms + add card (if not on file) on the call
- Check the email address where you send the quote

**2. Salesforce** *(if customer doesn't convert on call)*
- Move lead to **Decision Open**
- Log call outcome as **personal callback**
- Log detailed notes
- Update **external billing ID field** with: `Sales has sent quote` *(exact text)*

**3. Follow-up**
- Check quotes regularly — if one converts:
  - Update lead status from **Decision Open** to **Converted**
  - Book in the consultation call
  - Update any other forms/trackers

## Sales Process — Sale (Existing Customer)

⚠️ **Remember: check and confirm credit card is on file**

**Salesforce:**
- Log call as **successful sale** — lead status to **Converted** with converter date
- Select appropriate reason for the sale
- Update correct products sold
- Log detailed notes
- Update **external billing ID field** with: `Sale - please place order` *(exact text)*
- Ensure notes include:
  - Relevant details (subscription upgrade, old spon, etc.)
  - Confirmation customer agreed on the phone
  - Anything relevant for admin to place the order

**Then:** Update all other relevant systems, forms, booking calendars
